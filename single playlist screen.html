Arjit singh

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian singer and music composer.He sings predominantly in Hindi and Bengali but has also performed in various other Indian languages.He is the recipient of a National Award and a record six Filmfare Awards. He is also known as "King of playback singing".
